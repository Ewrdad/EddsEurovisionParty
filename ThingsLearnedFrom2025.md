# Things learned from 2025 app development

## 1. People are unlikely to engage

So very few people actually voted. This likely means that the application needs to be a spectacle on its own, and not rely on people to engage with it. Dynamic screens and things are needed to make it interesting to look at, and not just a voting app.

## 2. I suck at design

The design looked disjointed so this year I will leave it to AI to generate a seemless design. Doing that after the day lead to amazing results, so I will do it that way this year.

## 3. Session key was difficult

Last year I was so cautious and worried about security and finances but this year I am more confident and we can ditch the session key and just keep the app open. We can use geo restrictions and other depoyment techniques to do security. And ditch the session key entirely.
